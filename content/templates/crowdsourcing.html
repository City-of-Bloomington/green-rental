Crowdsourcing

Why?

Many cities have a rental database that track the rental properties for the city. Utility providers have records of the utility usage at a given address. In an ideal scenario, we'd take those two sources, mix them together, and, Voil√†, RentRocket!

In our experience, most utility companies are unable to provide utility data in bulk for rental properties. Customer privacy is a concern. We get that. Maybe that will change in the future, and we are happy to work with any utilities interested in sharing this information. For details, please see our partner page.

In the mean time we are moving ahead and collecting this information organically.

How?

We've worked hard to make it easy to share details about rental properties directly via RentRocket.org. Encourage others to do the same.

To really get things going, here are some techniques we've used to collect information in bulk:

  • Engage local universities and schools to collect information as a class project.
  • Host a data collection party.
  • Organize contests.
  • Attend housing fairs.

Please take these ideas and adapt them to your community. If you can think of any other ideas or suggestions, please let us know. {% endblock %} {# content #}
